About Us

Welcome to Andrew's Pet Sitting, your trusted partner in providing top-notch care for your furry family members in Lethbridge, Alberta!

At Andrew's Pet Sitting, we understand that your pets are not just animals; they are cherished members of your family. With a deep love and passion for animals, we are committed to ensuring that your pets receive the utmost care, attention, and love when you can't be there with them.

Our mission is simple: to provide reliable and compassionate pet sitting and dog walking services that allow you to have peace of mind while you're away from home. Whether you have a busy work schedule, travel frequently, or simply need some extra help caring for your pets, we've got you covered.
